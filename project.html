Zealicon App

The official android app of the annual fest of JSSATEN, Zealicon 2020. The theme of the fest is "Music". Explore all events day-wise as well as category-wise. Get all the upcoming events at one place. Set reminder to your favourite events. Register for any event, directly from the app.Tech used are Kotlin, Model-View-Viewmodel Architecture, Navigation component, Room database, Retrofit 2, Coroutines, GSON, Glide, Stetho, Lottie and Firebase Analytics.
